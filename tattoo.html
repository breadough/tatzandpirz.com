Preparation and Aftercare

Things to do before and after having a tattoo

Tattoo Preparation Checklist

Over the age of 18.

Not under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

Not pregnant or nursing.

Tattoo is permanent, think carefully of your tattoo design.

Have a good rest and eat a meal before the session.

Wear something that you will be comfortable, avoid wearing whites.

Tattoo Aftercare Do's:

Wash your hand before touching the tattoo.

Wash your tattoo with a mild antibacterial soap.

Dry your tattoo with a clean towel.

Keep your tattoo exposed as much as possible for quicker healing.

Wear loose-fitting clothing, If possible, to prevent the fabric from pressing against your tattoo.

Update your artist with the status.

Lastly, Wear your tattoo with pride.

Apply an anti-inflamatory balm to reduce swelling and redness.(Optional, for large tattoo)

Pain-relieving gels and creams may provide additional comfort.(Optional, for large tattoo)

Tattoo Aftercare Donts's:

Don't user hot water to clean your new tattoo; Wash with warm water instead.

Don't put your new tattoo directly under running water.

No swimming. Avoid soaking your tattoo to any fluid.

Don't pick the scabs.

Don't go tanning.
